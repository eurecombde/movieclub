Eurecom Movie Club

This is the homepage for the Eurecom Movie Club, a club for movie loving technologists studying at the French riviera.

Want to come watch a movie? We'd love to have you with us! The schedule is to show one movie every week, on Fridays around 18 if not other message is given. It's only 2 euros to join a movie, 5 if you want to have pizza and drinks included. You are of course free to bring your own candy as well! All movies are shown at the Eurecom Amphi.

Ferris Bueller's Day Off 14-03-2014

We're kicking it off this season with the greatest high school movie from the 80s, say hello to Ferris Bueller everyone!

Today it's quite common to shy far away from any movie set in a high school environments, but there was a time when they managed to make quality content with this theme. This is the highlight from that period.
